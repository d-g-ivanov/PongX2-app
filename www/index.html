Pong X Two

An oldie, but a goodie. And just as the classic, you may enjoy it with a friend!

Travel back in time to enjoy the simple graphics of one of the first games ever made.

Pong is a 2-dimensional simulation of (table) tennis. You and your opponent share a phone to each control an in-game paddle by moving it horizontally (and vertically, if you'd like) across the screen. You use the paddles to hit a ball back and forth. The aim is for each player to reach eleven points (default, but you can change that) before the opponent does; points are earned when one fails to return the ball to the other.

Check out the settings area for some personalization fun and extra fine-tuning.
